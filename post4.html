NATURAL INTELLIGENCE VIS-A-VIS ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE

NATURAL INTELLIGENCE is an offshoot of Closed System it is independent of Direction and Alternative both of which are generated by the Closed System from within. Whereas,

ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE is an offshoot of Open System and therefore depends on outside input for both Direction and Alternative. Thus just as Natural Intelligence, Artificial Intelligence can also be defined on same lines

“COMPUTER IS A DEVICE THAT SELECTS DATAS AS “ALTERNATIVES” EFFECTIVITY AND EFFICIENTLY ON THE BASIS OF PRE-PROGRAMMING”
Thus
TENDENCY is user.

ARE WE DESIGNING INTELLIGENT COMPUTERS?
OR
